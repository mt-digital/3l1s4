Will you know if they fall?

Anonymized supervision and automated monitoring with fall prevention

Features 3D Infrared, Wall-Mounted Sensors.

No wearables! No daily charging!

  • RoomMate detects & sends alerts for behavior that leads to falls & injuries
  • Reduces staffing requirements, and travel to people’s homes
  • Improves security, sleep and quality of life

The “smart” way to bridge the physical and digital worlds

  • RoomMate is a unique solution for looking after patients without intruding on their personal space
  • A wall mounted 3D sensor alerts about a variety of incidents without any other physical installations or wearables
  • Alerts are supported with an anonymized image and/or sound of the situation and the sensor can be used for two-way communication

Privacy By Design. Images are not stored, but all actions are logged.

Care providers receive alerts and alarms via the RoomMate app

Response to alerts and visual supervision directly from a smartphone or tablet

Intuitive user interface for managing zones, housing units, residents, care providers, sensors, alert rules and log files

Quick view control room display with real time resident status and metrics

A video guide library and user manuals for both care providers and administrators
